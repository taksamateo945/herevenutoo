Freshman quarterback De'Andre Johnson, who was in the mix for Florida State's starting job in 2015, was suspended indefinitely for a violation of athletic department policy on June 25.

Per ESPN.com's Mark Schlabach, however, the Jacksonville, Florida, native allegedly punched a woman in a bar.   

Schlabach reported the quarterback was charged with misdemeanor battery on June 30, and ESPN's Randy Scott reported a warrant had been issued for his arrest.
