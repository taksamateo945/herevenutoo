Seychelle Gabriel Bio; Parents, Education, Height

Seychelle Suzanne Gabriel was born on 25 March 1991 to parents Guy Gabriel and Michelle Gabriel. She grew up with four sisters and a younger brother Dylan DiForte Gabriel in Burbank, California.

Gabriel with a height of 5 feet 3 inches (1.6 meters) attended Burbank High School in Burbank, California. There, she graduated at the age of 18 in 2009.

Her Career; Movies, TV Shows, Net Worth

Seychelle began her acting carrer at the age of 16 from the comedy TV show Zoey 101 in 2007. She then was cast as Young Sand Saref in the 2008 superhero movie The Spirit. Eventually, she gained her prominency from her role as Princess Yue in the 2010 action-adventure fantasy movie The Last Airbender.

Gabriel appeared in a recurring role in the TV show Falling Skies and lent her voice in the animated series, The Legend of Korra. Some of her work in movies includes Honey 2 (2011), Sleight (2016) and Blood Fest (2018).
