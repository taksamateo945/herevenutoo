Drake is a Canadian rapper, singer and songwriter who became famous as an actor on the TV show Degrassi: The Next Generation. He is one of the most influential and popular artists in modern music, known for mixing singing and rapping in his songs and for bringing R&B sensibilities to hip-hop.

Career

Drake began his music career by releasing his first mixtape, Room for Improvement, in 2006, followed by Comeback Season in 2007 and So Far Gone in 2009. He then joined Young Money Entertainment, a label founded by Lil Wayne.

Drake has had cameo roles in films and TV shows such as Anchorman 2: The Legend Continues and Think Like a Man Too. He is a “global ambassador” of the Toronto Raptors NBA team and obtained naming rights to their practice facility.

Children

Drake has a son named Adonis Graham, who was born on 11 October 2017.

Family

Drake was born to Dennis and Sandi Graham. His parents split up when he was five years old, and he was raised by his mother in Toronto. Drake has a half-sister named Stephenie, who is from his father’s previous relationship.

Net Worth

According to Forbes, Drake’s net worth as of 2023 is estimated at $250 million. He earns most of his income from his music sales, streaming royalties, touring, endorsements and business ventures. He has deals with brands such as Apple Music, Sprite, Nike, Virgin America and Whataburger. He also owns the OVO Sound record label, the OVO fashion label and the Better World fragrance House.

Awards

Drake has won numerous awards and accolades for his music and achievements. He has won four Grammy Awards, six American Music Awards, 29 Billboard Music Awards, 20 BET Awards, six Juno Awards and three MTV Video Music Awards.
