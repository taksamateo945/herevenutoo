Woman dead after crash near LaVilla

JACKSONVILLE, Fla. – A woman died after a crash near Lavilla School of the Arts Sunday evening, according to the Jacksonville Sheriff’s Office.

An SUV and a minivan hit at the intersection of Kings Road and North Davis Street around 9:30 p.m., a report said.

According to JSO, there were two people in each vehicle and all four people were taken to the hospital. The woman, who was the passenger in the SUV, died from her injuries in the crash.

The JSO Traffic Homicide Unit is investigating.

This is the 108th traffic-related death this year in Duval County.
