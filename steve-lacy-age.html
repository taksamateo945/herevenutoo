Steve Lacy Age: How Old Is the Grammy-Nominated Singer-Songwriter?

Steve Lacy stands out as an exceptionally gifted and versatile artist within his contemporaries.

His remarkable talents span across singing, songwriting, guitar playing, and producing, with notable collaborations alongside renowned artists such as Kendrick Lamar, Solange, and Tyler, the Creator.

Additionally, he boasts an impressive solo discography, comprising two albums, namely “Apollo XXI” and “Gemini Rights,” both of which beautifully showcase his distinctive fusion of R&B, indie rock, funk, and psychedelic soul genres.

As we delve into this article, we will uncover details about Steve Lacy’s age, family background, net worth, and his significant accomplishments in his career.

Does Steve Lacy Have A Child?

Steve Lacy, born on May 23, 1998, in Compton, California, is presently 25 years old in the year 2023.

His diverse heritage includes an African-American mother and a Filipino father.

He spent his formative years alongside his sister, Asia Lacy, who is also a talented singer.

Tragically, his father passed away when Steve was just 10 years old, leaving their upbringing in the capable hands of their mother.

As of 2023, Steve Lacy remains childless, channeling his energies into his burgeoning music career and personal development.

He has courageously shared his sexual orientation, identifying as bisexual, and has previously dated both men and women.

However, at present, he has not disclosed any information about his current romantic relationships.

What Is Lacy Net Worth?

As of 2023, Steve Lacy boasts an estimated net worth of $35 million, primarily amassed through his multifaceted career in the music industry.

His financial success has been derived from his roles as a solo artist and a pivotal member of the band The Internet.

Additionally, he has augmented his income by engaging in fruitful collaborations with renowned artists, reaping the rewards of his songwriting and production talents.

Beyond music, Lacy has ventured into the world of modeling, lending his image to esteemed brands such as Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Marc Jacobs, and others.

Lacy’s journey in music commenced at a young age, where he harnessed the power of GarageBand on his iPhone and utilized an iRig plug-in for his guitar to produce songs.

It was in 2013 that he crossed paths with Jameel Bruner, an encounter that introduced him to the R&B collective, The Internet.

In 2015, he played a pivotal role in co-producing their third album, “Ego Death,” a project that garnered a Grammy nomination for Best Urban Contemporary Album.

Additionally, he marked his solo debut with the release of “Steve Lacy’s Demo” in 2017.

However, it was 2019 that saw Lacy’s meteoric rise to prominence with the launch of his inaugural solo album, “Apollo XXI,” earning him yet another Grammy nomination in the Best Urban Contemporary Album category.

The album featured tracks like “N Side,” “Playground,” and “Like Me,” which candidly explored his sexuality and identity.

Furthermore, he lent his musical prowess to Vampire Weekend’s “Sunflower” in the same year through a collaborative effort.

Fast forward to 2022, and Lacy unveiled his second solo album, “Gemini Rights,” which impressively secured the sixth position on the Billboard 200 chart.

The album spawned the chart-topping sensation, “Bad Habit,” which soared to number one on the Billboard Hot 100, thanks in part to its virality on TikTok.

This remarkable achievement led to Lacy receiving two Grammy nominations, one for Record of the Year and another for Song of the Year in 2023.

Moreover, his musical talents extended to a collaboration with Calvin Harris on the track “Live Without Your Love” in 2022.

How Much Did Steve Lacy Sell First Week?

As per Billboard’s data, Steve Lacy’s “Gemini Rights” achieved remarkable sales figures in its debut week of release in July 2028, with a total of 86,000 equivalent album units sold.

Out of this total, album sales accounted for 54,000 units, while streaming equivalent albums (SEA) contributed 31,000 units.

This outstanding performance secured the album’s debut at the impressive sixth position on the Billboard 200 chart, marking Steve Lacy’s inaugural entry into the top 10 as a solo artist.

Additionally, “Gemini Rights” demonstrated strong performance on various other charts.

It claimed the number one spot on the Top R&B Albums chart and reached the second position on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart.

Moreover, it secured the fourth spot on the Top Album Sales chart and the fifth position on the Billboard Global Excl. U.S. chart.

Conclusion

Steve Lacy, a 25-year-old artist, has achieved success with two acclaimed solo albums, Grammy nominations, and collaborations with industry giants.

His net worth is estimated at $35 million in 2023, making him a highly influential and innovative artist of his generation.

No children.
