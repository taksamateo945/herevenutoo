Kendrick Lamar Announces European Tour Dates

Kendrick Lamar has announced he is to play a series of live dates across Europe in 2018.

The tour, in support of Lamar’s most recent album ‘DAMN.’, kicks off in Dublin on February 7th before visiting Birmingham, Manchester, Glasgow, London then heads over to mainland Europe.

All 15 shows on the tour will be supported by Mercury Prize winner James Blake.

Check out the full tour dates below:

February 9th: Birmingham
February 10th: Manchester
February 11th: Glasgow

March 5th: Berlin

30 year old Kendrick Lamar released his fourth studio album ‘DAMN.’ in April of this year.

The album, which features the singles ‘Humble’, ‘DNA’ and ‘Loyalty’, hit the no. 1 spot on the Billboard 200 chart upon it’s release.

Tickets for all UK and Ireland dates go on sale this Friday, October 6th.
