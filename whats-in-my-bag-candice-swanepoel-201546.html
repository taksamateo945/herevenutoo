What's In My Bag: Candice Swanepoel

For Victoria’s Secret Angel Candice Swanepoel, jet-setting is a way of life. “I have a lot of old boarding passes just randomly in there,” the 26-year-old South African beauty, who has graced the covers of magazines in more than a dozen countries, says of her Alexander Wang tote. “I also have pesos From Mexico and reals from Brazil. I need to do a clear-out!” The trilingual star, (dating Brazilian model Hermann Nicoli) empties her purse for Us.
