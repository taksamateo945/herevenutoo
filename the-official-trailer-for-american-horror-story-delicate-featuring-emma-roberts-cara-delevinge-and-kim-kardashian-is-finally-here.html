Delicate featuring Emma Roberts, Cara Delevinge, and Kim Kardashian is Finally Here Celeb Sec

Emma Roberts, Cara Delevinge, and Kim Kardashian are coming together to star in the upcoming season of American Horror Story: Delicate. 

Until this point, fans have only seen quick glimpses into this upcoming season through still pictures and short teasers, but with an official trailer on our hands, fans have a MUCH better idea of what this highly-anticpated season will entail. 

According to The Hollywood Reporter, this season is based on a novel — Danielle Valentine’s Delicate Condition. The novel is described as “a gripping thriller about an actress (Roberts in the show) who becomes convinced that a sinister figure is going to great lengths to make sure her pregnancy never happens.” The book has been called a feminist update to Rosemary’s Baby.

In the two minute clip, Roberts’ character seems to be the main focus. Her persona seems to channel a celebrity in the spotlight who is struggling with her pregnancy journey, while Kardashian’s character steps in to try to bring comfort to her *possible client* yet, that is not enough for the young woman with nightmares.

We also see Cara Delevinge’s character develop as a stalker and also a potential nurse in the hospital who has an attachment towards Roberts. It’s filled with creepy doctors, bloody gowns and haunted lullabies making us even more excited to the big debut later this month. 

American Horror Story: Delicate will be split into two parts, with part one dropping September 20 on FX, streaming the next day on Hulu.
