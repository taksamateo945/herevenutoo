Lauren Jauregui reveals her current favourite lip colour...and it's a Kylie Lip Kit!

Fifth Harmony beauty Lauren Jauregui is famous for her edgy make-up looks.

She loves a brown lip and took to Twitter to inform her followers about which lip product is her go-to at the moment. Lauren accompanied her Tweet with an effortlessly gorgeous selfie and added that she can’t seem to stop wearing Kylie Jenner’s Lip Kit in the shade ‘Dolce K.’

Kylie’s Lip Kits have been a big hit with, not only famous faces, but pretty much the whole world, so it’s no surprise that Lauren loves it too. She also said that she “wears it every day,” and we reckon we would too if it would make us look half as amazing as Miss Jauregui!

Ngl Dolce K by @kyliecosmetics is my favorite color rn I wear it every day no lie I luv it pic.twitter.com/LS8NIsRJ6I

— Lauren Jauregui (@LaurenJauregui) August 5, 2016

Lauren is often seen rocking bold brows, neutral eyes with a cat eye flick and matte brown lipstick, we guess you could say it’s become her signature look!

If you want to recreate Lauren’s look, then you can check out this tutorial which the girls’ make-up artist filmed last year when she was doing Lauren’s make-up.

And if you want to copy Lauren’s exact look and get your hands on ‘Dolce K,’ be sure to keep your eyes peeled on Kylie Jenner’s Twitter, Instagram, App and KylieCosmetics website to ensure you don’t miss out on when the next restock is! Those lippies go like hot cakes so you’ll have to be FAST!
