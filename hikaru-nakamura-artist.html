Hikaru Nakamura Biography

Hikaru Nakamura is one of the most popular and richest Mangaka who was born on April 21, 1984 in Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan. Hikaru Nakamura (中村 光 , Nakamura Hikaru) (born April 21, 1984 in Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan) is a Japanese manga artist. She is mostly known for Saint Young Men and Arakawa Under the Bridge. A Nikkei Entertainment magazine article published in August 2011 listed her ninth overall among the top 50 manga creators by sales since 2010, with 5.54 million copies sold. She is married to Hiroshi Kamiya and they have a child together.
