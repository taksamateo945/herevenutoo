Tony Stewart Wife Leah Pruett: Explore Their Married Life, Age, Kids, And Family

Tony Stewart and Leah Pruett married in Los Cabos, Mexico, on November 21, 2021. The racing driver couple engaged on March 19, 2021.

The couple engaged privately, it was only revealed when Leah was seen flaunting her giant diamond ring, and Tony shared a post writing that he is excited to spend the rest of his life with her.

Tony Stewart and Leah Pruett live in Hidden Hollow Ranch, 415 wooded acres of property in Columbus, Indiana.

The race car driving lovebirds have a similar interest in racing. They are incredibly supportive of each other’s careers.

Pruett and Stewart are parents to a fur baby, Mia, and happily living in Columbus, Indiana. They do not have any children.

The couple is seen enjoying vacations to Daytona. Stewart has frequented NHRA races since he and Pruett started dating last year.

Pruett began racing Jr. Dragsters as a child and progressed through Nostalgia Funny Car, NHRA Pro Mod, and, since 2013, Top Fuel.

Leah Pruett’s husband Stewart began racing go-karts as a child and has since accomplished about all there is to achieve in circular track racing.

In the spring of 2021, Leah Pruett was featured on the cover of DRAG ILLUSTRATED for the third time, alongside her husband, Tony Stewart.

These images were taken with renowned photographer Rick Belden during a multi-day photo session at zMax Dragway in Charlotte, North Carolina.
