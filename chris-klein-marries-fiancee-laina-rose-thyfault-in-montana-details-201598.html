Chris Klein Marries Fiancee Laina Rose Thyfault in Montana: Details!

Here’s to the newlyweds! Chris Klein married his fiancee, Laina Rose Thyfault, in Montana over the weekend, his rep confirms to Us Weekly. The couple exchanged vows on Sunday, Aug. 9, in front of 115 guests at the Rainbow Ranch on the banks of the Gallatin River.

Klein wore a Shoreditch London three-piece suit to tie the knot. 

Us confirmed in December 2014 that the American Pie actor, 36, popped the question to travel agent Thyfault in Venice Beach after three years of dating.
